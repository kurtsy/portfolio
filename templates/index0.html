Hi, I’m Kurt. I’m a Brooklyn-based designer pursuing an M.S. in Information Experience Design at Pratt Institute. I studied journalism and urban design at NYU as well as user experience design at General Assembly. Designer is a tricky word but I used it to describe myself because I enjoy turning visions into realities. I also wield a whiteboard marker like a wizard. I prefer to explore as many solutions to a problem as possible before focusing on a single idea.

I avoid applying formulas to design problems and allow evidence and context to drive my decision-making. My favorite part of the design process is eroding long-held assumptions in the light of strong user research. It’s those lightbulb-exploding, "Aha" moments (and lots of caffeine) that keeps me energized on a daily basis. Want to turn some on lights together? Feel free to reach out by email, download my resume, or check out my work by clicking any of the colorful circles.

Design is a war for the truth. It's about fighting through egos, misconceptions, and stalemates to emerge with something beautiful.

I live for lightbulb-bursting revelations, but I know they don't happen by magic. Honest solutions require teamwork, deep understanding, and evidence-based decisions. Spirited explorations that leave hands marker-stained and walls covered in post-its work, too.

I'm currently pursuing an M.S. in information experience design at Pratt Institute. I studied journalism and urban design at NYU. Reach out by email, download my resume, or check out some of my work by clicking on the colorful circles.

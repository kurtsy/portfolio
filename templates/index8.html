OVERVIEW

I designed and coded this site myself. I made sure it abides by the ADA standards for accessible design. By clicking or tabbing to the circular nodes, you can unlock some of my work.

My portfolio is inspired by the idea that we can attain enlightenment by tapping into our best energy. I believe design requires the same level of awareness as a spiritual practice and insight should guide teams to build meaningful digital experiences. I hope you find some of the work here inspiring.

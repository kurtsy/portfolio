OVERVIEW

My portfolio was inspired by the seven chakras, an idea that we can attain enlightenment by breaking down barriers and tapping into our best energies. I believe design requires the same level of effort as spiritual awareness to build memorable and meaningful digital experiences.

I designed and coded this portfolio site myself. Care and love was put into building a fully accessible website that abides by ADA compliance.
